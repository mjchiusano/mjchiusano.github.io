Mark Chiusano is the author of Marine Park, a collection of stories. His fiction and essays have appeared in Guernica, Narrative Magazine, Harvard Review, and online at Tin House and The Paris Review, among other places. He was born and raised in Brooklyn, educated at Harvard College, spent some summer playing baseball in Switzerland, among other places sometimes more exotic than the Parade Grounds, and now works at Vintage Books.
